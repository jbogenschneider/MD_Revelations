# MD_Revelations
This is a 2026 submod for Millennium Dawn for Hearts of Iron 4 (HOI4). This mod took what the 2025 mod started and brought the "bookmark" up to 2026 as most of the countries were more like 2017 or still stuck on 2000. This will be maintained and will be updated independently within an MD release cycle and ideally immediately following a release.

This mod presents the 2026 world with real life events and close to a timeline to this point.  New focus trees with a ton of thought and balancing for Ukraine, Russia, USA, Canada and Nato Countries.   The secondary purpose of this mode is to have a realistic Ukraine - Russia situation that can wrap that conflict up.  The primary purpose of this scenario is to play Trump's USA.

The mod is called Revelations 2026 because the unthinkable is now possible, the USA could be in a war against NATO within the next 3 years.  It's not that I think the current president wants to go to war with NATO or plans to, I think that he expects them always to back down.  This mod lets you play that what if.  Canada is a particularily fun country to play as you could really thwart the USA with a stiff defense.

You can do the other things too, like bomb Iran, start a civil war, or put troops on the ground in Iran.  Same for Cuba.

Russia has some "new path" options, you'll want to check out.

China, Japan and other non-nato countries might have been updated some by the 2025 mod, China in particular.

The focus trees will take you to about 2029, so I might go beyound that.  There's still a lot of events to mostly "turn off", but they shouldn't get in the way.

I'm not trying to build a full Mod like Millennium Dawn is, this will stay a submod around the main theme of USA vs NATO, WW3.  But it will affect other nations and their opportunities, China in particular.

Desclaimer:  I'm using the premis of End Times Prophesy to create a fun to play HOI4 scenario of WW3.  In the loading screens I provide generally acknowledged scripture that describe an anti-Christ, as well as, recent quotes from people in line with that theme.  If you aren't into that, then you can divert your eyeballs.  I did want to raise awareness to the fact that US geo-political decisions today are being heavily influenced by people with End Times on their mind.  My opinion is that is bad and not consistent with what Jesus asked us to do, which is to be loving and caring and not dividing and fighting.
